## Clarifying Product Names

There appears to be a slight confusion in the naming of AI memory management products.

- **mem0** (by mem0.ai): A well-known open-source memory management layer for AI agents.
- **TeamMem**: There is no widely recognized standalone product called exactly "TeamMem".

It is highly likely that comparisons involving "TeamMem" are actually referring to **Microsoft Teams Memory** (the built-in knowledge feature).

## Product Summary

- **mem0.ai**: Focused on open-source memory layers for LLM agents.
- **Microsoft Teams Memory**: Integrated enterprise knowledge management within Microsoft Teams.
